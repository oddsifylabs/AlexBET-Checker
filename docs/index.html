Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AlexBET Checker.

How do I get access to the bot?

AlexBET Checker is exclusive to Testudo Legio members. Join via Whop and you'll get instant access to the bot along with all other community benefits.

What sports are supported?

Currently NBA only. NFL, MLB, NHL, and soccer support is in development and coming soon to members.

Does it check spreads and totals?

Right now, AlexBET Checker supports moneyline results only. Spread and over/under checking is in active development.

What formats does it accept?

Multiple formats: Lakers 4/30/2026, GSW 04-30-2026, nyk 4/30/2026, Golden State 2026-04-30. Team aliases, abbreviations, and various date formats all work.

Is my data stored?

No. Your bet checks are ephemeral and private. We don't store your queries or link them to your Telegram account.

What if the game is in progress?

The bot will show "In Progress" status with the current score. Check back after the game ends for the final result.

Can I use this on desktop?

Yes! Telegram works on iOS, Android, desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux), and web. The bot works everywhere Telegram does.
